How it Works

You + Friends + Philz.

  • 1

    Join your school's Philz Group

    All your friends who also like Philz will be there too! (So all the cool kids)

  • Option 1

    Pick up your friends' drinks!

    With none of the pain! They put in their order, pay on their device, and all you have to do is pick it up. You can notify your friends when you're going to Philz, and the more friends you pick up drinks for, the bigger the discount. The drinks will be ready soon after you arrive, so no need to worry about waiting longer.

  • Option 2

    I want Philz, but don't want to drive.

    Good thing your friends are going! Find who's going to Philz and when. If it works out, tag your order onto theirs.

  • 3

    Meet up with Friends!

    Whether you just picked up the order, or stayed on campus, everyone on the order gets to meet up and share some delicious Philz. Friends + Philz = Better Friends
